About Us

The story behind Acme Co.

Our Story

Acme Co. began in 2015 as a small workshop with one simple idea: make things people will actually keep. Our founder grew tired of disposable product and wanted to bring back the quality of an warlier era ─ when things were built to last and made by hands that cared.

Our Mission

To craft everyday goods that earn their place in life ─ through quality materials, honest design, and the kind of finish you can feel in your hands.

Our Values

  1. Carft ─ We design slowly and bouid carfully. Shortcuts cost more than they save.
  2. Honesty ─ We tell you what something is made of, where it came form, and what is costs us to make.
  3. Longevity ─ Products should outlive trends. Reparirs should be possible. We answer every email ourselves
